I know it’s difficult  <br>
I know it’s hard <br>
I know you miss me <br>
And so do I <br>

I never promised it was going to be easy <br>
I never am <br>
I never promised it will all be rainbows and sunshines <br>
But I promised I will always love you and here I am <br>
